Why Love Mini Dog?

Mini dog has many admirable features, one including the paws. The paws are an essential feauture of the mini as these are his way of transportation. He also has a prestine nose that glimmers in the sunlight.

What Does Mini Look Like?

Mini dog has short legs and little toes. He also has silky soft fur and two black marble eyes. He has white and caramel fur. The bottom of his feet are no bigger than a quarter.

How to Keep Mini Dog Happy

Mini dog requires one good walk a day. Otherwise he will make whatever room he pleases as his toliet. He also likes a fresh bowl of water each morning, or else he will not drink. Often causing dehydration. Lastly, several treats are required per day ranging from meatballs to chicken to ice cream.
